 1. A method for displaying text information, comprising:displaying a list of at least one item representing text information and a partial content of the text information; andmodifying, in response to a predetermined key input, the list to show whole content of the text information corresponding to the at least one item in the list.
    2. The method of claim 1, wherein the list comprises a first icon.
    3. The method of claim 2, wherein modifying comprises expanding, when a touch is made to the first icon, the at least one item in a predetermined direction to present the whole content of the text information.
    4. The method of claim 2, wherein modifying comprises changing the first icon for a second icon.
    5. The method of claim 4, further comprising:monitoring to detect an input to the second icon; andshrinking, when an input to the second icon is detected, the at least one item in an opposite direction to present the partial content of the text information.
    6. The method of claim 1, wherein the at least one item comprises an omission symbol.
    7. The method of claim 2, wherein displaying comprises:extracting the partial content from the text information;ordering the at least one item having a third icon in the predetermined direction; andpresenting the list of the at least one item having the partial content along with the first icon.
    8. The method of claim 7, wherein modifying comprises expanding, when an input to the third icon is detected, the item having the third icon to which the input is detected in a predefined direction to present the whole content of the text information and, when an input to the first icon is detected, the at least one item a direction to present the whole content of the text information.
    9. The method of claim 8, wherein modifying comprises changing the third icon for a fourth icon and changing the first icon for the second icon.
    10. A text information display method comprising:displaying a list of a plurality of items of text information and a portion of the text information for each of the plurality of items;selecting at least one of the items according to a user input; anddeleting, when an predetermined key is input, the at least one selected item.
    11. The method of claim 10, wherein displaying comprises presenting a first icon on the list.
    12. The method of claim 11, wherein displaying comprises presenting, when an input to the first icon is detected, a second icon in each of the items.
    13. The method of claim 12, wherein selecting comprises changing the second icon of the at least one selected item in shape.
    14. The method of claim 10, wherein displaying comprises configuring each item with whole content of the text information, ordering the items having third icons in a predetermined direction, and presenting the list having the first icon.
    15. The method of claim 14, further comprising, when an input to a first icon is detected, changing the third icons of the items for the second icons.
